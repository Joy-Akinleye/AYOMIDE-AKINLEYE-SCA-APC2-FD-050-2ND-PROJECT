MY JOURNEY

My Journey into Tech - website development specifically started in 2019, just right after secondary school. I wanted to take a programming class at a Training center but the programming tutor didn't show up for 2 weeks and i decided to join the Website Development class where i was introduced to HTML & CSS. It turned out to be fun but i didn't stay for long, hence no serious study/learning was done. Fast forward to university days when i saw people making money from it and decided to re-study it and make it a profession but it was hard to combine with school work but i was trying bit by bit. I started learning JavaScript in 2023, during my SIWES Training but it was more of theory and JavaScript is a little bit tough so i gave up but here i am now at SHE CODE AFRICA, putting all myself and brain together to get it right.
